about

Fig 1.1: The Author

I am Aryaman. I build systems, largely to see whether the universe objects.

This site is a catalogue of those negotiations. Experiments in mathematics, hardware, and code, undertaken not because they are especially useful, but because they seem just barely permissible. Each project begins with a set of rules, proceeds under the assumption that those rules are incomplete, and ends either in success or in a clearer understanding of why success was never on the table.

It is, in practice, a register of minor improbabilities. A record of occasions on which logic behaves impeccably, reality cooperates reluctantly, and precision is achieved in spite of everything. The aim is not to impose order, which would be unreasonable, but to locate pockets of clarity and examine them before they collapse back into noise.

The work is guided by a simple curiosity: why, in a universe so committed to disorder, anything works at all.
